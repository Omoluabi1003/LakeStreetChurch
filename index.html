About Lake Street

Lake Street Church of Christ in Stuart, Florida, is a vibrant community dedicated to faith, fellowship, and service. Our mission is to spread the love of Christ through worship, education, and outreach.

Join us to experience God's love and become part of our family. We welcome all with open hearts and open doors.

Our Ministries

Outreach Ministry

Our Outreach Ministry is all about spreading joy and making a difference in our community, because we know that doors open on the hinges of love! With energetic engagement and heartfelt support, we're on a mission to share hope and kindness, creating uplifting connections that empower everyone we meet!

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry empowers young individuals to deepen their relationship with God in a welcoming environment designed for exploration and growth. We confidently open doors that swing on the hinges of love, inviting youth to connect, learn, and thrive in their spiritual journey.

Senior Ministry

Our Senior Ministry is dedicated to honoring and caring for the seniors in our church family. We provide spiritual support, fellowship, and various activities tailored to the needs and interests of our senior members. Join us as we cherish the wisdom and experience of our senior community.

Acts of Service

Community Service Program

Be a part of our dynamic Community Service program, where we enthusiastically feed homeless and participate in inspiring ministry! Together, we build meaningful connections offer support that empowers individuals and brings hope to our community!

Baptism Service

Welcome to our church! We believe in the significance of Baptism as a public declaration of one's faith in Jesus Christ. It's a beautiful symbol of the spiritual cleansing and rebirth. Join us for our Baptism service and take this important step in your spiritual journey.

Communion

At Lake Street Church of Christ, we come together to partake in the sacred act of Communion. Join us as we remember the sacrifice of Jesus through the sharing of bread and wine, symbolizing His body and blood. It's a time of reflection, gratitude, and unity with our church family.

Marriage Services

Celebrate the sacred union of marriage at Lake Street Church of Christ. Our church provides a beautiful and spiritual setting for couples to exchange vows and seek God's blessings for their life together. We offer pre-marital counseling and wedding ceremony services to support couples in this special journey.

Confession & Repentance

Experience the healing power of confession and repentance in a safe and supportive environment. Our church provides opportunities for individuals to confess their sins, seek forgiveness, and find spiritual renewal. Join us for our confession and repentance sessions to experience God's grace and love.

Prayer for Healing

Experience the power of prayer and healing at Lake Street Church of Christ. We offer prayer for the sick and anointing with oil, as well as laying on of hands for those seeking physical, emotional, or spiritual healing. Join us for our healing prayer services and find comfort and restoration in God's presence.
